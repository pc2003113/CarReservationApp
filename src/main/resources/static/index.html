Car Reservation App

The Car Reservation App is a web based car booking site for customers to rent a car

In order to use this app, the customer will have to first register, create a profile and login to the app for making the reservation of the selected car

The following are are the available features of this website:-

  1. Customer Registeration
  2. Customer Login
  3. Display of list of cars
  4. Make Reservation
  5. email confirmation with booking number
